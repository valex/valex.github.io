Binance Moonbix Game

Looking for a fun way to earn rewards while gaming? Binance has launched Moonbix, a new play-to-earn (P2E) game available directly on Telegram. Set in space, players control a spaceship with a claw to collect valuable items scattered across galaxies. Despite an early leak, the game is now being fine-tuned for an official release, and excitement is building within the crypto and gaming communities. With Binance's reputation for innovation and security, Moonbix promises an exciting mix of entertainment and real-world rewards. Stay tuned for the official launch, and join the adventure!

When you start playing Moonbix, you're given 100 coins and six attempts to master the game. Your goal? To catch golden balls and avoid pesky meteors, all within 45 seconds per try. The game is similar to classic claw machines, where timing is everything. Don’t worry about running out of chances—your attempts regenerate one every 10 minutes, keeping the action going.

What makes Moonbix even more exciting is its built-in referral program. Invite friends, and they’ll get 1,000 coins, while you earn 10% of their winnings, up to 50,000 coins. Plus, completing tasks like subscribing to Telegram channels or linking your Binance account can boost your score and unlock even more opportunities for rewards.

The more points you rack up, the higher your chances of earning crypto rewards. However, these rewards are only available to users in qualified regions who’ve linked their Binance account and completed KYC verification. Binance has made it clear that Moonbix is not affiliated with Telegram or any other social media platforms, ensuring a secure and independent gaming experience.

Start your journey through the galaxy today and discover how fun and rewarding crypto gaming can be!
